What is a social enterprise?

It is a business that sells goods or services and aims to make a profit. But what sets them apart from private businesses is that they invest their profits into addressing a social problem in their community.


A local example of this is Our Power Energy.

They are a new energy supply company established by Scottish social housing providers who want to make the energy market work better for their residents and communities.

Where can I find a list of social enterprise retailers?

If you're in Edinburgh you can search the directory on the Edinburgh Social Enterprise Website.

This enables you to filter by business type, for example:

  • Busiess Services
  • Food and Drink
  • Creative
  • Sport and Leisure
